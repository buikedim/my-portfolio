echoVC Portfolio Management System

echoVC, a Venture Capital company wanted a flexible platform for managing their wide portfolio of clients and investments. Areas like report scheduling, dashboard sharing and portfolio company information management required better flow.

TEAM

Charles Dim - UI Designer || Janet John - Front-end Dev || McDonald Aladi, Abiodun Agbolade, Eniola Agboola - Back-end Dev

Research

Early phase research involved gathering information about their workplace heirarchy to determine user access and also have a good idea of current platforms both pms and otherwise being used by the company.

Pain points

Pain points expressed by stakeholders included the inability to forward reports to more than one portfolio company at a time which the software they were using at that time did not offer. Also expressed was the lack of a way to manage different fund periods. All these led to coming up with features for the platform and some include:

Suggested features

All these led to coming up with features for the platform and some of them include:

  • Report Scheduling to enable selection of more than one recepient at diferent receipt times
  • Tabbed funding periods for a general view of events within that period
  • Backup capability
  • Investor relations to take notes
  • Investee company management to ensure information is up to date
  • Metric and KPI representation means
  • Contacts management
